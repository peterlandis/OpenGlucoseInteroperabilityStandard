# Governance

OGIS and OGT are open standards developed through community collaboration.

## Principles

- open participation
- transparent decision-making
- vendor neutrality
- practical, implementable standards

## Decision Making

- small changes can be merged via pull requests
- major changes require RFCs
- maintainers guide direction and resolve disputes

## Maintainers

Initially, the project is maintained by the founder.

Over time, maintainers may expand to include:

- device manufacturers
- developers
- healthcare experts
- community contributors

## Goal

To evolve into a widely adopted open standard supported by a diverse ecosystem.
